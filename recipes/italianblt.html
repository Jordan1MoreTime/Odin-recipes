Italian BLT

Here, we see an Italian blt.

The Italian BLT is just like a normal BLT except that is it Italian. So we will need some italian Ingredients

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • tomato
  • Spices

Steps to make the italian blt

  • Toast the bread
  • Add the gabagol or bacon to the bread
  • Add the lettuce, basil, olive oil, and tomatos
  • Lastly, add the mozzarella and enjoy
