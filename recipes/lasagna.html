Lasagna

Here, we see Garfield eating lasagna.

Lasagna consists of pasta, cheese, and sauce. The pasta is flat and paperlike yet thicc and flavorful. The cheese types are feta and monzeralla.
And the sauce is tomatoe based with your choice to add meat or not.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Sause

Steps to make Lasagna

  • Make the sauce
  • Cook the pasta and meat
  • Layer it pasta, sauce, then cheese.Pasta, sauce, cheese.
  • Then bake for 20min at 375. remove from oven; and let cool and enjoy
