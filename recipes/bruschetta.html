Bruschetta

Here, we see Bruschetta.

Bruschetta consists of many thing. Mainly bread, olive oil, and tomatos.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Olive oil
  • tomato
  • basil
  • galic
  • mozzarella

Steps to make bruschetta

  • Make galic bread
  • Add tomatos on top
  • drizzle balsomic on top
  • Then bake for 10min at 375. remove from oven; and let cool and enjoy
